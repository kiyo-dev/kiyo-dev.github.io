About

Built by one person

I'm an indie developer based in Bangkok, Thailand, building apps that solve my own problems — and hopefully yours too.


Most of my apps are born from frustrations with existing tools. Whether it's a radio app that actually records, a board viewer that works across multiple sites, or a party game you can play at a bar — each app starts with a simple "why doesn't this exist?"


Built with Swift, SwiftUI, and SwiftData, deeply integrated with Apple frameworks like CloudKit, CarPlay, WidgetKit, and Live Activities.

Privacy Policy

My apps respect your privacy. They do not collect personal data, do not track you, and do not share information with third parties. Some apps use local storage (iCloud sync, SwiftData) solely for your convenience — your data stays yours.


For app-specific privacy details, please refer to each app's listing on the App Store.
