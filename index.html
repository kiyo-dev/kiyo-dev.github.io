About

Built by one person

I'm an indie developer based in Bangkok, Thailand, building apps that solve my own problems — and hopefully yours too.


Most of my apps are born from frustrations with existing tools. Whether it's a board viewer that works across multiple sites, a party game you can play at a bar, or a three-way AI decision engine — each app starts with a simple "why doesn't this exist?"


Built with Swift, SwiftUI, and SwiftData, deeply integrated with Apple frameworks like CloudKit, CarPlay, WidgetKit, and Live Activities.
